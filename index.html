About A:M

More Than
A Session

A:M Volleyball is a community-driven coaching organisation operating across Manchester and Newcastle. Founded and led by Alex Monk, the mission is simple: bridge the gap between social and competitive volleyball.

"I want to foster a community that helps players bridge the gap between being a social player to being one that can compete competitively."
Skills Lab Game Sessions Tournaments Camps Clinics Personal Coaching 7am League

Founder & Head Coach

Alex Monk

Alex has built A:M Volleyball from the ground up into one of the North of England's fastest-growing volleyball communities. His game-based approach focuses on technique, fast play, and strong fundamentals, the same principles he applies coaching at the competitive level.

  • Current coach of an NVL Division 1 team
  • Coached university teams up to Division 1 level
  • Organised Manchester's largest ever indoor volleyball tournament
  • Game-based approach: technique, speed, strong fundamentals
  • 1 on 1 personal coaching available

Player Development

What You'll
Gain at A:M

Six months with A:M Volleyball produces a measurable difference. Here's what players consistently walk away with.

🎯
Technical Ability

A clear, noticeable increase in ball control, serving, setting, passing, and hitting, built through structured, purposeful reps.

🧠
Tactical Understanding

Greater game sense, smarter positioning, and an understanding of how to read and respond to play. Areas most coaches skip over.

🏐
Ready to Compete

Improvements directly relevant to joining a team during the season. A:M prepares you for real, competitive volleyball.

💪
Focused Coaching

Specific attention on the areas other coaches don't address. The gaps that hold most players back from levelling up.

"A player who has come to A:M can expect a greater tactical understanding of the sport : positioning, game sense, and specific focus on areas other coaches don't build on."

Alex Monk, Founder

Player Levels

Train at the
Right Intensity

A:M uses a structured levelling system to help players progress step by step. Levels are carefully assigned and open to discussion.

1
🏐

Level 1

Beginner

New to indoor volleyball. Focus on building fundamentals and confidence on the court.

2
⚡

Level 2

Developing

Good grasp of indoor volleyball. Decent ball control or strong athleticism ready to be refined.

3
🏆

Level 3

Advanced

In-depth understanding of indoor volleyball. Strong ball control and athleticism, or exceptional in one area.

Levels are open to discussion, reach out via Spond or Instagram DM
